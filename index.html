Cycle Tracking

With our app, you can track and predict your period, ovulation and lets you know your chances of becoming pregnant on a specific day as well as the chances you are pregnant based on your use of birth control and sexual activity. Among other things you can also track when you have sex, your moods, weight and discharge.



Birth Control Alarm

Birth control reminders can be sent via text or email. Users can choose when to get alerts. You can reply to the message stating that you have taken your birth control and it will log what time you take it and no longer send you reminders for the day.



Compare Birth Controls

As a new or current user, one can choose to compare different birth control methods based on symptoms. As you log symptoms, we will suggest birth control methods for you to try based on what has worked for other people with similar symptoms.
